What is sleep deprivation?

Sleep deprivation occurs when an individual does not get enough sleep, either in duration or quality, to function properly during waking hours. It can be caused by various factors such as insomnia, sleep disorders, lifestyle choices, work schedules (like shift work), stress, or medical conditions.

Symptoms of sleep deprivation can include

  1. Excessive daytime sleepiness
  2. Difficulty concentrating
  3. Impaired memory
  4. Mood changes
  5. Irritability
